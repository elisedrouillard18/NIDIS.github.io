Empowering Communities for a Resilient Sahel

Our Mission

The Network for Inclusive Development in the Sahel (NIDS) is an apolitical, non-profit and humanitarian organization, sub-regional, which works to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly engaged in sustainable development programs, the fight against against climate change, renewable energies, the environment and health.

Our work focuses on vulnerable people and communities. We carry out advocacy, awareness-raising and concrete actions leading to adaptation and resilience of communities to crises. We work in five (5) Sahel countries; Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger, Mauritania and Chad with local communities made up of women, young people, displaced populations and indigenous peoples.
