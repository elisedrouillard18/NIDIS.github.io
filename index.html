Our Mission

The Network for Inclusive Development in the Sahel (NIDIS) is an apolitical, non-profit and humanitarian organization, operating in the Sahel Region. It focuses on achieving the Sustainable Development Goals, with a particular emphasis on climate change mitigation, renewable energies, environmental conservation, and health initiatives.

Our efforts concentrate on supporting vulnerable individuals and communities, employing advocacy, awareness campaigns, and practical initiatives to enhance community resilience in times of crisis. Operating within five Sahel countries—Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger, Mauritania, and Chad—we collaborate closely with local communities comprising women, youth, displaced populations, and indigenous groups.
