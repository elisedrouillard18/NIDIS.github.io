Our Mission

NIDIS (Network for Inclusive Development in Sahel) is dedicated to fostering sustainable development across the Sahel region, with a focus on advancing the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Since its inception in 2018, initially operating through volunteer efforts, NIDIS has evolved into a full-fledged organization by 2020. Since its establishment NIDIS has helped over 100,000 people through a variety of efforts. With a multifaceted approach, NIDIS tackles various critical issues plaguing the Sahel, including development, humanitarian aid, food security, and access to clean water.

Through collaborative initiatives and community-driven projects, NIDIS strives to empower local communities, promote economic growth, and enhance resilience against environmental challenges. NIDIS prioritizes its efforts towards the most vulnerable communities within the Sahel region, ensuring that their voices are heard and their needs are met. By addressing these fundamental needs and championing inclusivity, NIDIS endeavors to create lasting positive change in the Sahel, fostering a future of prosperity and well-being for all its inhabitants.

What Sets Us Apart

NIDIS stands out among NGOs in Africa for its unique approach to community development and its unwavering focus on helping vulnerable populations. What sets NIDIS apart is not only its commitment to making a difference but also its innovative strategies and dedicated team of young individuals aged 18 to 28.

Unlike other organizations, NIDIS harnesses the power of volunteers to mitigate costs, ensuring that donations directly impact those in need. Moreover, NIDIS doesn't just provide immediate aid; it invests time and effort into setting up communities for long-term success. Before implementing projects like solar-powered water systems or agricultural initiatives, NIDIS conducts extensive groundwork, ensuring that communities are ready for sustainable change.

But what truly sets NIDIS apart is its deep connection with the communities it serves. With members living in rural areas across the region, NIDIS understands the unique needs and challenges of these populations. This firsthand knowledge allows NIDIS to tailor its projects effectively, ensuring maximum impact.

By supporting NIDIS, you're not just donating to another charity; you're investing in transformative change. NIDIS doesn't simply alleviate immediate hardships; it takes communities from struggling to survive to prospering in a matter of a few years. Your donation to NIDIS isn't just a gift; it's a catalyst for lasting prosperity and positive change in vulnerable communities across Africa. Join us in supporting NIDIS and be a part of this incredible journey towards sustainable development and brighter futures for all.

Our 5 Year Plan

In 2024-2025, our ambitious endeavor involves the creation of a large solar-powered water tower in a desperate community, poised to provide access to water for over 10,000 individuals and facilitate farming and irrigation needs. As we embark on this transformative project, we invite sponsors to be integral partners in our journey. Beyond financial contributions, sponsors will be engaged in the project's development, kept informed of milestones, and provided opportunities to contribute expertise and resources. Your sponsorship and support will be instrumental in realizing this vision, creating lasting impact and sustainable change within the community.

• NIDIS aims to expand efforts to more remote communities and increase the number of volunteers, primarily focusing on enhancing funding through partnerships and sponsorships.

• Initial investment in water projects to address water scarcity issues, freeing communities from the burden of long journeys for fresh water.

• Implementation of agricultural projects post-water stabilization, empowering women to engage in crop cultivation and support their families.

• Community empowerment through teaching effective farming techniques for previously unfarmable land, guiding them towards self-sustainability.

• Workshop initiatives in schools to educate children on renewable energy, inspiring them to become future change-makers in their communities.
