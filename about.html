Our Mission

NIDIS (Network for Inclusive Development in Sahel) is dedicated to fostering sustainable development across the Sahel region, with a focus on advancing the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Since its inception in 2018, initially operating through volunteer efforts, NIDIS evolved into a full-fledged organization in 2020. Since its establishment NIDIS has helped hundreds of thousands of people through a variety of efforts. With a multifaceted approach, NIDIS tackles various critical issues plaguing the Sahel region, including development, humanitarian aid, food security, and access to clean water.

Through collaborative initiatives and community- driven projects, NIDIS strives to empower local communities, promote economic growth, and enhance resilience against environmental challenges. NIDIS prioritizes its efforts towards the most vulnerable communities within the Sahel region, ensuring that their voices are heard, and their needs are met. By addressing these fundamental needs and promoting inclusivity, NIDIS strives to create lasting positive change in the Sahel region, fostering a future of prosperity and well- being for all its inhabitants.



Meet the Team

What Sets Us Apart

NIDIS stands out among NGOs in Africa for its unique approach to community development and its unwavering focus to help vulnerable populations. What sets NIDIS apart is not only its commitment to making a difference but also its innovative strategies and dedicated team of young individuals aged 18 to 28.

Unlike other organizations, NIDIS harnesses the power of volunteers in the villages to mitigate costs, ensuring that donations directly impact those in need. Moreover, NIDIS doesn't just provide immediate aid; it invests time and effort into setting up communities for long-term success. Before implementing projects like solar- powered water systems or agricultural initiatives, NIDIS conducts extensive groundwork, ensuring that communities are prepared for sustainable change. NIDIS doesn't simply alleviate immediate hardships; it takes communities from struggling, to surviving, to prospering in a few years through a combination of initiatives.

But what truly sets NIDIS apart is its deep connection with the communities it serves. NIDIS staff and volunteers have close ties to the communities they assist, either living or hailing from these areas, fostering a strong bond and understanding the local needs. This firsthand knowledge allows NIDIS to tailor its projects effectively, ensuring maximum impact.

Our Plans for the Future

For the 2024-2025 period, our organization is committed to the construction of a substantial solar-powered water infrastructure in a critically underserved community. This initiative is projected to secure reliable water access for more than 10,000 residents, significantly enhancing agricultural productivity and irrigation capabilities. Contributions from sponsors will go beyond mere financial assistance; we seek to foster an environment of active engagement, ensuring our partners are well-informed of progress and are given the opportunity to lend their specialized knowledge and resources. The assistance and involvement of our sponsors are essential to the fulfillment of this project, which promises to create enduring benefits and promote sustainable development within the community.

Water Project Expansion: We are extending our reach to new communities, aiming to implement large-scale solar-powered water towers in desperate areas. These projects will provide access to clean water for over 10,000 individuals, addressing water scarcity and facilitating agricultural needs.

Reproductive Health Initiative: Recognizing the importance of reproductive health in empowering women, we are launching a comprehensive initiative. This program focuses on providing reproductive aid, offering education on safe sex practices, and promoting overall reproductive well-being.

International Engagement: We are establishing an international presence by connecting with new partners across the globe. Through collaboration and partnerships, we aim to amplify our impact and reach more communities in need.

Educational Workshops: We are investing in the future generation by conducting workshops in schools. These workshops focus on renewable energy education, inspiring children to become future leaders and change-makers in their communities.
